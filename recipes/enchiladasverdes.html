Enchiladas Verdes

Description

Enchiladas Verdes are a delicious Mexican dish made with corn tortillas filled with chicken or cheese, then topped with a tangy green salsa made from tomatillos and cilantro. This dish is comforting and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (or cheese for a vegetarian option)
  • 2 cups green salsa (salsa verde)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (like queso fresco or Monterey Jack)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. If using, sauté the diced onion until translucent.
  3. Add the shredded chicken (or cheese) to the skillet and mix with half of the green salsa. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Lightly warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave to make them pliable.
  5. Spoon the chicken mixture into each tortilla, roll them up, and place them seam-side down in a greased baking dish.
  6. Pour the remaining green salsa over the enchiladas and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until heated through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot, drizzled with sour cream and garnished with fresh cilantro.
